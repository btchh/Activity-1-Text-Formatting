My Story

My name is Paul Lourence F. Reguis, though online I refer to myself as d.btchh_. I was born on October 25th, 2005, in the small town of Angono, Rizal, but as of now, I live with my family in Rosario, Batangas. One of my biggest dreams is to become a virtuoso violist, though right now my focus is simply on surviving college life.

My mom was a former OFW in Hong Kong. She returned to the Philippines in 2013 to personally take care of me and my brother. Meanwhile, my father stayed in Angono, Rizal, working as both a tricycle driver and a mechanic to support us. It was my uncle who had the biggest influence on my academic path—he’s an IT professional and encouraged me to pursue Information Technology in college, assuring me that it would be easier to secure a decent job with his guidance.

My academic journey began in 2008, when I enrolled in Christ the King College of Angono, where I completed Kinder 1 & 2 and Grade 1. In Grade 2, I briefly attended Joaquin Guido Elementary School, before transferring to BPES, where I finished my elementary education. Later, I studied at SNFSS-NC, completing both Junior High and Senior High under the STEM strand.

Outside academics, I am passionate about my music. One of my favorite playlists is Mood Swings , which I often listen to while relaxing or studying. I also enjoy playing League of Legends and Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB). In my free time, I love reading yaoi and bara while listening to music.

I started learning the viola and violin in 2020, but I paused my practice in 2023 due to personal reasons. These days, I’ve shifted my focus to the ukulele, which brings me a different kind of joy and creativity.

As of now, I honestly have no clear goal for my future. I’m still figuring things out step by step, and that’s okay. One thing I am certain about, however, is that I am planning on getting married not planning on getting married. For now, I simply want to focus on myself, enjoy the things I love, and see where life takes me.
